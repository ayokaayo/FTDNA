Token Pipeline & Architecture

FastTrack uses a sophisticated design token pipeline built on Design Tokens Studio. Our tokens are organized into three layers: Brand Colors (primary palette), Semantic Colors (functional meaning), and Component-Specific Tokens (implementation details). This architecture enables flexible theming, consistent styling across platforms, and easy updates that cascade throughout the entire system. All tokens are code-generated into CSS custom properties, ensuring single-source-of-truth for design across web and mobile platforms.

Design-Development Alignment

Our design system is built on the principle of single-source-of-truth. 839 Figma components are meticulously crafted by the design team, with 20 of the most critical ones already implemented in production code. This measured approach ensures both design fidelity and development feasibility. New components are prioritized based on usage frequency and design impact, with the entire roadmap tracked and visible to both teams.
